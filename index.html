About Me

I am a Site Reliability Engineer with over five years of experience ensuring uptime, performance, and operational excellence across enterprise production environments in regulated financial services.

At BNP Paribas, I owned on-call incident response using PagerDuty, built full-stack observability with Dynatrace across 4,000+ servers, and automated deployments across 30,000+ servers using Python and Ansible — saving 180+ hours and reducing manual effort by 85%.

My approach to reliability is simple: every alert should be actionable, every deployment should be reversible, and every incident should make the system stronger. I focus on building observability that prevents outages, automation that eliminates toil, and processes that make on-call sustainable.

I hold an MSc from Cranfield University and have recently completed the McKinsey Forward Programme. I am currently pursuing AWS Solutions Architect Associate and CKA certifications.
